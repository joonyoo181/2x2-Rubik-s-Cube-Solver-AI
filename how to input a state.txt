When inputting the cube state, type the colors of all of the stickers as capital letters.
For a solved cube, the state would get entered as 'BBBBOOOORRRRYYYYWWWWGGGG'
In the example above, each sequence of 4 characters represents 1 face of the cube.
The cube faces are entered in this order:
1. Front
2. Left
3. Right
4. Up
5. Down
6. Back
